What have we been up too?

As a company, we place an importance on knowledge, look and see how we've been expanding as a company

A.I is here, and we love every bit of it! We've been paying attention to A.I every since the release of Chat GPT, and while most have seen the recent craze as a problem for the job market we've been taking advantage of every program. We have been able to improve not only our video production, but also our overall productivity thanks to different A.I programs and we look forward to seeing where the future with this technology takes us.

We've moved! Change is difficult, however we have adjusted quite well. For those who do not know we have moved back home to the islands of The Bahamas. While we did enjoy our time spent in the United States. We were blessed with an opportunity to return home and expand our business. While we will miss all of the friends we've made, we know this is not the last we will see of them! Until then we will be focusing on the stories of the creatives and business of the beautiful islands of the Bahamas.

Look forward to a production upgrade! Here at Blessed Cinema, we take our quality of work very seriously. We have taken the time to look over our footage, and have noticed that it's time for us to increase our quality to keep up with, what is desired from our clients and the market. So we have invested heavily into not only our video but also our lighting and audio equipment. We look forward to implementing a new standard of visual quality
