"Everyday is a Chance to Create a New Memory"

How it all began...

Based in New Providence, Bahamas. Johnathan Albury was inspired by a college friends to pick up a camera and start making videos, it was a instant connection. He quickly established a loved for visuals and the story he could tell. With over a two years of experience he has been able to create videos for weddings, DJ Concert, Music Videos, and Social Media Promotion for college sports. “Every Create Something New!” A saying that gives purpose, everyday we wake up is a opportunity to create and share memories. Starting his brand Blessed Cinema, Johnathan inspires to continue to use his gift to tell the stories of those around him and highlight their greatest moments.
